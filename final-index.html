ABOUT AIIMS BATHINDA

All India Institute of Medical Sciences, Bathinda is a prestigious medical institution located in the city of Bathinda, Punjab, India. Established to provide top-notch healthcare and medical education, AIIMS Bathinda was established under the Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suraksha Yojna in 2019. Our institution is equipped with state-of-the-art medical facilities, cutting-edge research centers, and a team of highly skilled healthcare professionals and researchers. AIIMS Bathinda stands among the top 10 medical colleges nation-wide in ADR reporting. It stands strong with its 35 departments and learned faculty whose sole motto is to promote better healthcare for all.
